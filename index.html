About

Introducing our AI-powered novel sentence to image generation API platform service. With our platform, you can generate high-quality images based on sentences from novels. By setting the characteristics and background of the protagonist in advance, you can ensure consistent quality. You can also input pre-existing images to extract keywords related to the protagonist, which can further enhance the quality of the generated images.

Features

  • AI-powered image generation based on novel sentences
  • Customizable protagonist characteristics and background
  • Keyword extraction from pre-existing images
  • High-quality and consistent image output
